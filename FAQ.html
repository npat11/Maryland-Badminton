Frequently Asked Questions

We welcome any current student, staff, or faculty member of the University of Maryland - College Park into our club.

All practices are held in the gym in the School of Public Health (SPH2101).
Practices are 9:00 p.m. - 11:30 p.m. on Tuesdays and Fridays, and 5:00 p.m. - 8:00 p.m. on Saturdays.

We require everyone to register on TerpLink and IMLeagues prior to attending practices. You can find information on how to do this here. Once you complete both of these registrations, you may attend our practices.

You are only required to bring your student id (UID). We would recommend bring a water bottle and closed-toe shoes. Additionally, if you do have equipment that you would like to bring feel free to do so, but don't worry if you don't have any as we provide rackets and shuttles to anyone who wants to borrow.

We post all of our announcements on our social media profiles (Instagram, Facebook, and Discord). Be sure to follow them to stay up to date.

The competitive team competes in the Eastern Collegiate Championships, which occur once each semester, as well as other local, open tournaments.
In order to join the team, we have a specific process which we use. More information about this can be found on our team page.
