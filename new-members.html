New Members

With over 400 members on TerpLink, we are one of the largest club sports on campus. Additionally, with 10 courts available for play, attending practices is a great way to meet new people while also engaging in some great games in a very low stakes and low commitment environment. Don't worry if you don't have rackets and/or shuttles as we provide them to anyone who needs them for free. All you should bring is a pair of closed-toe shoes (preferably court shoes), water, your UID, and a positive attitude!


While you are free to come to as many or as few practices as you would like, we require that everyone registers on both TerpLink and IMLeagues in order to attend

TerpLink Registration

  1. Navigate to TerpLink and sign in
  2. Search for Maryland Badminton
  3. Select the option under the organizations section
  4. Click the join button on the top-right of the screen

*We will receive a message and will approve your membership into the club soon once these steps have been completed

IMLeagues Registration

  1. Navigate to IMLeagues and sign in using your UMD credentials
  2. Find Club Badminton
  3. Watch the concussion video and pass the 9 question Quiz
  4. Sign the electronic waiver
  5. Submit Athlete Personal Information
