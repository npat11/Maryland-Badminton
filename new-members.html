New Members

With over 400 members on TerpLink, we are one of the largest club sports on campus. With 10 courts, attending practices is a great way to meet new people while also engaging in some great games in a very low stakes and low commitment environment. Don't worry if you don't have rackets and/or shuttles as we provide them to anyone who needs them for free.


While you are free to come to as many or as few practices as you would like, we require that everyone registers on both TerpLink and IMLeagues

TerpLink Registration

Navigate to Maryland Badminton on TerpLink and click Join in the top-right corner. This will send us a message and we will approve your membership into the club.

*You must be signed into TerpLink to register
